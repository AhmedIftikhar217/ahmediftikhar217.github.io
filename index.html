Combating Climate Change

Problem Statement

Climate change is one of the most urgent challenges of our generation. Rising global temperatures, increasing greenhouse gas emissions, and unsustainable consumption patterns threaten ecosystems, communities, and future generations. Our project asks: what can we do to combat climate change? We explore practical, technology-driven solutions — from renewable energy and sustainable transportation to individual lifestyle changes — that individuals, communities, and governments can adopt to reduce carbon emissions and move toward the goal of net-zero by 2050.

Paper Prototype

Based on our personas and brainstorming, we built a paper prototype of "ClimateAction" — a mobile app that helps users take small daily steps to reduce their carbon footprint. The prototype includes six core screens: onboarding, dashboard, challenge detail, progress tracking, community feed, and action completion.

User Testing Tasks for Next Week
